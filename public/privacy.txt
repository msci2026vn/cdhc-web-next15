CHINH SACH BAO MAT - CON DUONG HUU CO (CDHC)
=============================================

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 2026-01-09

1. THONG TIN THU THAP
---------------------
Chung toi thu thap cac thong tin sau:
- Thong tin ca nhan: Ho ten, email, so dien thoai
- Thong tin tai khoan: Google OAuth (email, ten, anh dai dien)
- Thong tin su dung: Cookies, du lieu phan tich

2. SU DUNG THONG TIN
--------------------
Thong tin cua ban duoc su dung de:
- Cung cap va cai thien dich vu
- Xac thuc nguoi dung
- Gui thong bao quan trong
- Phan tich va cai thien trai nghiem nguoi dung

3. BAO VE THONG TIN
-------------------
Chung toi su dung cac bien phap bao mat:
- Ma hoa SSL/TLS cho tat ca ket noi
- Luu tru an toan voi HttpOnly cookies
- Kiem soat truy cap nghiem ngat
- Xac thuc token JWT

4. CHIA SE THONG TIN
--------------------
Chung toi KHONG ban hoac chia se thong tin ca nhan cua ban cho ben thu ba,
ngoai tru:
- Khi co yeu cau phap ly
- De bao ve quyen loi cua CDHC
- Voi su dong y cua ban

5. QUYEN CUA BAN
----------------
Ban co quyen:
- Truy cap thong tin ca nhan
- Chinh sua thong tin
- Xoa tai khoan
- Tu choi nhan thong bao marketing

6. COOKIES
----------
Website su dung cookies de:
- Duy tri phien dang nhap
- Nho tuy chon cua ban
- Phan tich luong truy cap
