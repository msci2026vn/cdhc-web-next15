DIEU KHOAN SU DUNG - CON DUONG HUU CO (CDHC)
=============================================

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 2026-01-09

1. CHAP NHAN DIEU KHOAN
-----------------------
Bang viec su dung dich vu CDHC, ban dong y voi cac dieu khoan sau.
Neu khong dong y, vui long khong su dung dich vu.

2. DANG KY TAI KHOAN
--------------------
- Ban phai cung cap thong tin chinh xac
- Ban chiu trach nhiem bao mat tai khoan
- Moi nguoi chi duoc tao mot tai khoan
- Phai du 18 tuoi hoac co su dong y cua phu huynh

3. SU DUNG DICH VU
------------------
Ban dong y KHONG:
- Vi pham phap luat Viet Nam
- Spam hoac gui noi dung doc hai
- Can thiep vao hoat dong cua he thong
- Su dung bot hoac cong cu tu dong trai phep
- Gia mao nguoi khac

4. NOI DUNG NGUOI DUNG
----------------------
- Ban giu quyen so huu noi dung cua minh
- Ban cap cho CDHC quyen su dung noi dung de cung cap dich vu
- Noi dung phai tuan thu phap luat
- CDHC co quyen go bo noi dung vi pham

5. DIEM VA PHAN THUONG
----------------------
- Diem OGN va TOR chi co gia tri trong he thong CDHC
- Khong duoc mua ban, chuyen nhuong diem trai phep
- CDHC co quyen dieu chinh ty le quy doi

6. TAI KHOAN DOANH NGHIEP/HTX
-----------------------------
- Can xac minh thong tin doanh nghiep
- Phai duoc Admin phe duyet
- Chiu trach nhiem ve thong tin san pham dang ban

7. CHAM DUT
-----------
Chung toi co quyen dinh chi hoac xoa tai khoan:
- Vi pham dieu khoan su dung
- Hoat dong gian lan
- Theo yeu cau phap ly

8. GIOI HAN TRACH NHIEM
-----------------------
CDHC khong chiu trach nhiem ve:
- Thiet hai gian tiep tu viec su dung dich vu
- Mat mat du lieu do loi nguoi dung
- Gian doan dich vu do nguyen nhan khach quan

9. THAY DOI DIEU KHOAN
----------------------
Chung toi co the cap nhat dieu khoan bat ky luc nao.
Viec tiep tuc su dung dich vu dong nghia voi viec chap nhan thay doi.

10. LIEN HE
-----------
Neu co thac mac ve dieu khoan su dung:
- Email: support@cdhc.vn
- Website: https://cdhc.vn
